It's Not Easy Being... A DJ

From the swamps to the stage, Feral Frequencies (aka Kermit the Frog) has been dropping beats that make even Miss Piggy want to dance. What started as ribbiting rhythms in the pond has evolved into a full-blown EDM phenomenon.

With a unique blend of swampy basslines and lily pad melodies, Feral Frequencies creates a sound that's unmistakably amphibian. His sets take you on a journey from the deepest marshes to the brightest festival stages.
